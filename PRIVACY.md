## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing activity.

## Local Storage

The extension stores three user preferences locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API:

- Preferred video quality level
- Theatre mode toggle (on/off)
- Premium enhanced bitrate toggle (on/off)

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to the extension developer or any third party.

## Permissions

- **Host permission** (`*://www.youtube.com/*`): Required to inject content scripts that interact with the YouTube video player. The extension only runs on YouTube pages.
- **Storage permission**: Required to save your preferences locally.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.
